#### Objective

This section provides a short video walkthrough of CloudDoc in action, showing the full document lifecycle end to end — from upload, through moderation, to search, preview, and download — so the reader can see the implementation working beyond static screenshots.

#### Demo video

📹 **Watch the demo:** [CloudDoc.mp4 (Google Drive)](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1-mvKrbzs08WWHUT0-whSGeNwgtwd4Uid?usp=sharing)

#### What the video covers

- Uploading a document from the frontend and tracking the upload progress
- Verifying the object and metadata after the upload completes
- Reviewing and approving the document from the admin/moderation area
- Searching for the approved document and using preview/download
